Association for Computing Machinery

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947 and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.
The Students of NSRIT computer department are affiliated with a society like ACM. Student Society events are conducted every 2 weeks. And These events are organized by the students under the ACM banner. Clink on the button below to visit the ACM event page.
